The beggining of airplanes industry became the faster evolution of a transport machine since the industrial revolution. Varig

1927 - 2006

Rio Grande do Sul

Varig became in Rio Grande do Sul opperating flights from the city of Porto Alegre to Rio Grande, after a time became the biggest of further south region of Brazil

Vasp

1933 - 2005

São Paulo

Vasp, well known as the airline of São Paulo state, became as the first state airline conecting in 1936 SP to RIO state

Transbrasil

1955 - 2001

Santa Catarina

Transbrasil didn't became as a passengers company, it started as "Sadia Transportes Aéreos", used to send fresh meat from Santa Catarina to São Paulo, after a fast lift, became passengers company and expanded to the entire country
